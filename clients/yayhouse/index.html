Our Story

Made with love in Surrey since 2019

Yayhouse was born from a simple idea: a place where you actually want to stay. Not just for coffee — but for the feeling.

We source our beans from ethical, small-batch roasters, bake with local suppliers, and make every drink with care. Whether you're in for five minutes or five hours, this is your spot.
